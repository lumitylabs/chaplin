## ğŸ Known Issues

Below is a transparent list of issues we have identified and are on our radar to fix.

- **Limited Authentication:** Currently, login is exclusively via Metamask.
- **Response Latency:** Responses from Chaplins with multiple agents may experience slowness.
- **Incomplete Favorites Feature:** The option to save Chaplins as favorites is not yet persistent across sessions.
- **Chaplin Management:** It is not currently possible to edit or remove Chaplins after their creation.
- **Chaplin Privacy:** All created Chaplins are public by default, with no option to make them private.
